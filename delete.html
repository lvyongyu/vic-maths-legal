Request data deletion — Vic Maths

App: Vic Maths: Curriculum Practice · Developer: Yongyu Lyu · Last updated: 23 June 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of the data associated with the Vic Maths: Curriculum Practice app.

How to request deletion

You can remove your data in either of these ways:

  1. On your device (clears local data immediately): open the app → Settings → Reset progress and Settings → Reset profile, or simply uninstall the app.
  2. To delete your cloud-synced copy: email lvyongyu@gmail.com with the subject "Vic Maths data deletion request". We will delete your cloud data and confirm by reply. Because the app uses an anonymous identifier (no name or email is stored), please send the request from the device or include any reference shown in the app's Settings so we can locate the correct record.

What data is deleted

On request we permanently delete the cloud-stored data linked to your anonymous identifier in Google Firebase / Cloud Firestore, including:

  • Your selected year level
  • Your practice progress and mistakes book
  • App settings and any recorded purchase entitlements

The app does not store your name, email, phone number, or any other personal identifier, so there is no such data to delete.

What is kept, and for how long

  • Cloud data: deleted within 30 days of a verified request; nothing is retained afterwards.
  • Purchase records held by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play) are controlled by those stores under their own policies and are not stored on our servers.
  • On-device data is removed immediately when you use Reset or uninstall the app.

See also our Privacy Policy.
