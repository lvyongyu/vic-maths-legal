Vic Maths — Delete Your Data

Last updated: 24 June 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of the data that Vic Maths (developer: Yongyu Lyu) stores for you. Vic Maths uses no traditional account: you are signed in anonymously, and your data is linked only to a random, anonymous identifier — never to your name, email, or any real-world identity.

Delete your data from inside the app (fastest)

  1. Open Vic Maths.
  2. Go to the Me tab → Settings.
  3. Tap Delete cloud data and confirm.

This permanently deletes the copy of your progress that is synced to our servers.

Or request deletion by email

Email lvyongyu@gmail.com with the subject “Delete my data”. We will delete your cloud-synced data within 30 days and reply to confirm.

What is deleted

  • The random anonymous identifier used to sync your data
  • Your synced practice progress and mistakes book
  • Your selected year level, app settings, and purchase entitlement stored in the cloud

What is kept

  • Nothing identifying you is kept — we never collected your name, email, phone number, location, or contacts.
  • Records of purchases are held by Google Play / Apple under their own policies; manage those through your store account.
  • Data stored only on your device is removed when you uninstall the app.

See our Privacy Policy for more detail.
