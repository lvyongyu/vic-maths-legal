Vic Maths — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 June 2026

Vic Maths (“the app”) is a maths practice app aligned to the Victorian Curriculum. We designed it to collect no personal information. This policy explains what that means.

Information we collect

None that personally identifies you. The app has no account, no login, and asks for no email, phone number, or address.

The only information the app stores is kept locally on your device and never leaves it:

  • An optional display name you may type during setup (can be left blank).
  • Your selected year level.
  • Your practice progress and mistakes book (which questions you answered).

A random local identifier is generated on your device to keep your settings together. It is not linked to any real-world identity and is not transmitted.

How information is used

Only to run the app on your device: to show your year level, track your progress, and build your mistakes book. We do not transmit, sell, or share any of it, because none of it leaves your device.

Data sharing and third parties

  • No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.
  • No servers operated by us receive your data.
  • In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play). We never see or store your payment details. Please see Apple’s / Google’s privacy policies for how they handle purchases.

Children’s privacy

The app is intended for a general audience and is purchased and managed by parents/guardians. Because we collect no personal information from anyone — including children — there is nothing to disclose, share, or delete from a server.

Your control over your data

All data is on your device. You can clear it at any time:

  • Settings → Reset progress (clears stats and mistakes book), or
  • Settings → Reset profile (clears name and level), or
  • Deleting the app removes all stored data.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy; the “Last updated” date will change accordingly.
