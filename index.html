Vic Maths — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 June 2026

Vic Maths (“the app”) is a maths practice app aligned to the Victorian Curriculum. We collect no information that personally identifies you — no name, email, phone number, or address is required. This policy explains the limited data the app stores and syncs so that your progress is backed up.

Information we collect

The app has no traditional account and never asks for your email, phone number, or address.

To back up your progress and sync it across devices, the app signs you in anonymously using Firebase Authentication. This creates a random identifier that is not linked to your real-world identity.

Linked to that random identifier, the following is stored in the cloud (Google Firebase / Cloud Firestore):

  • Your selected year level
  • Your practice progress and mistakes book
  • App settings and any purchase entitlements

Your optional display name stays on your device only and is not uploaded.

How information is used

Only to run the app and sync your data: to restore your year level, practice progress and mistakes book on this and your other devices. We do not use it for advertising or profiling.

Data storage and third parties

  • Cloud data is stored using Google Firebase (Authentication and Cloud Firestore), acting as our data processor. See Google’s Privacy Policy for how they handle it.
  • No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not sell or share your data for marketing.
  • In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play). We never see or store your payment details.

Children’s privacy

This app is designed for school students, including children, and is intended to be installed and managed by a parent or guardian. We have designed it to comply with the Google Play Families policy and child-privacy laws such as the US Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

We do not collect personal information from children — no name, email, phone number, photos, location, or contacts. The optional display name stays on the device and is never uploaded. The only identifier used is a random, anonymous ID created by Firebase Authentication, used solely to back up and restore that user’s own practice progress (“support for internal operations”). It is not used for advertising, profiling, or tracking, and is not shared with third parties for marketing.

The app contains no advertising, no in-app browser, and no social or chat features.

Your control over your data

  • Settings → Delete cloud data permanently deletes the copy of your progress synced to our servers, from inside the app. See our data deletion instructions.
  • Settings → Reset progress clears your stats and mistakes book.
  • Settings → Reset profile clears your name and year level.
  • You can also email us at the address below to request deletion of your cloud data.
  • Deleting the app removes all data stored on your device.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy; the “Last updated” date will change accordingly.
